Hey my beans!

It's me again. Nope, it's **not** AI, like Wile-E-Coyote endlessly pursuing the roadrunner, trying to "capture" my essence. My essence is uncapturable. Is that even a word? Doesn't matter.

It’s a Monday morning. You’re at your desk, sipping coffee from your favorite mug. You’ve just finished your 3rd email, furrowing your brow in disbelief trying to understand why your colleague just escalated to your boss. You literally received that request like 2 hours ago.. I mean, really?

Out of nowhere, the floor begins to shake. Windows rattle. Fluorescent lights sway from the ceiling. Motivational poster frames crash to the ground - goodbye “hang in there” cat, you hung in as long as you could.

A black hole envelops the floor, racing towards your chair, swallowing everything in its path. You shoot a panicked glance towards your coworkers, oblivious, going about their morning routines. Coffee pours out of your mug like a caffeinated waterfall as you’re sucked into the void, plunging towards oblivion. You scream, but as Ridley Scott made absolutely clear, in space, nobody can hear you scream.

Moments later you awake, resting comfortably in a chaise lounge, your toes sinking into silky white sand, a wide-brimmed hat protecting your eyes from a beaming sun, a bedazzled, umbrella-adorned daiquiri glass forming drips of moisture around your fingers.

That’s right, you’re on holiday - yay!

Does any of this feel familiar?

For many of us, holidays are amazing, but they're also jarring. We're escaping the monotony of everyday life and finding ourselves in new, exciting, and unpredictable situations. Somehow, the break from routine resets our perspective and recharges our batteries. Vacations restore order to our lives. But the details, planning, and expense can destroy our sanity before our well-deserved holiday even starts.

Flights, hotels, meals, activities, travel budgets, and the list goes on. What can beanies do to help reduce that burden, even a bit?

Well, check out our newest feature: **travel plans**.

Build family (or personal - we're not judging) trips, collaborate on vacation ideas with your family, get helpful hints to improve or fix the gaps in your planning, and more. We even allow you to save pending or tentative plans with less than full detail. Because, if you're anything like me, you _never_ have the full detail until literally the last minute.
