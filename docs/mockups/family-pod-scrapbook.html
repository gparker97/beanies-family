Tree nuts

Food allergy · diagnosed 2021
⚠️ Severe · anaphylaxis risk
Foods to avoid
Walnuts, almonds, cashews, pecans, pistachios, hazelnuts, brazil nuts. Peanuts are a legume and are OK.
Reaction symptoms
Hives within minutes, throat tightness, wheezing, vomiting. Has had anaphylaxis once (2022).
Diagnosed by
Dr. Rachel Kim, pediatric allergist · last reviewed Mar 2026
Be careful with
Anything "made in a facility that also processes tree nuts." Always check labels. Baked goods from bakeries — ask.
🥥 coconut? (still checking) 🍫 hazelnut spreads 🥐 pastries w/ almond 🥗 pesto
Emergency response
1. EpiPen Jr. (0.15mg) in front pocket of backpack and in the kitchen drawer. 2. Call 911 immediately after administering. 3. Benadryl (12.5mg) for mild cross-contact only.

Notes

Grandma's Bolognese

the sauce that started it all 🍝

🕐 4h total 🍽️ serves 6 🌿 12 ingredients ⭐ 9 steps 🍳 cooked 14 times · ★★★★★ avg
Loved by
🧒🏼 Neil 👨🏻 Greg

🌿 Ingredients

  • ground beef (80/20)500g
  • pancetta, diced100g
  • onion, finely chopped1 large
  • carrot, grated1 medium
  • celery, diced1 stalk
  • garlic, minced3 cloves
  • tomato passata700ml
  • whole milk250ml
  • dry red wine250ml
  • bay leaf2
  • salt + pepperto taste
  • pappardelle500g

📝 Steps

  1. 1

    In a heavy pot over low heat, render the pancetta until golden. Don't rush this — the fat is the foundation.

  2. 2

    Add onion, carrot, celery. Sweat for 10 minutes until softened and translucent.

  3. 3

    Add garlic. Cook 1 minute more.

  4. 4

    Push vegetables to the side. Brown the ground beef in the middle.

  5. 5

    Pour in the milk. Simmer until absorbed, ~15 minutes. This is the secret.

  6. 6

    Add the wine. Simmer until evaporated, ~20 minutes.

  7. 7

    Add passata, bay leaves, salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer.

  8. 8

    Simmer uncovered for 2.5–3 hours, stirring every 20 minutes.

  9. 9

    Serve over pappardelle with a generous grating of parmigiano reggiano.

Grandma's notes

"never use a big pot for a little sauce. if it's too thick, add a splash of pasta water. if the kids complain, tell them to eat it anyway."
