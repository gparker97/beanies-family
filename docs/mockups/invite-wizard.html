What changes vs today

  • Two screens, one job each. Step 1 = "who". Step 2 = "send". No stacked helper paragraphs on either.
  • Explicit confirmation. Checkbox under the email gates the primary button — non-technical users can't autopilot through with the wrong account.
  • FAQ on demand. Encryption + little beanies + no-Google explanations live behind Questions or worries?, not on the default screen.
  • Per-bean share unified. Tapping a bean's share button opens this same wizard with the email pre-filled but checkbox unchecked — they actively confirm.
  • QR is the centerpiece of Step 2. Always visible, large, with corner brackets to nudge the eye — non-technical users will scan without reading. Messaging grid sits below "or send a link".
  • Warmth. Cream gradient frame, hugging-bean progress mark, Heritage→Terracotta CTA, dashed-border confirm card, "every bean counts" foot.
