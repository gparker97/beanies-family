final spec & open calls

what to lock in before this becomes Vue.

Pattern law

  • Cluster bloom = add. Grid layout, ＋ seal on every bean, lives behind the FAB. Never used for navigation.
  • Pure bean stack = navigate. Single column, no ＋ seals, lives behind the bottom nav. Never used for adding.
  • The seal does the talking. No need for "add" vs. "go to" microcopy — the visual difference is consistent enough.

Stack rules

  • Cap at 6. 5 visible items + 1 ＋N-more bean if the category has more.
  • Active route at the bottom. Closest to the thumb, ringed in Heritage Orange. The remaining beans stack upward in canonical (sidebar) order.
  • Eyebrow ribbon. Tiny pill above the stack — "🌳 planning · 3". Confirms the tap landed in the right place. Fades in last.
  • Same-tab tap closes. Scrim, Esc, scroll, back gesture also close.
  • Reduced motion. Spring stagger replaced by a 250ms fade.

Open calls

  • Naming. "Planning" vs "Treehouse" — Treehouse is brand-true but lengthier; Planning is shorter and more functional. Greg to call.
  • Money tab visibility. Hide entirely when canViewFinances is off → bar collapses to 3 tabs; grid stretches.
  • Settings & help. Stay in the existing top-right avatar menu. Not in the bar.
  • The "more" bean's overflow popover. Could be a right-anchored mini popover (shown), or a bottom sheet. Sheet wins on touch ergonomics if any future category grows past ~10 items; popover is lighter for the merely-9 case. Worth revisiting once a category actually hits 7.
